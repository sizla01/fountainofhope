Hope for clean water

Fighting the daily challenge for young girls ,moms and sons of getting clean water has been our Primary goal from the very first day. Our feet are on ground working with communities around and other organization to lift this burden.

Providing a reliable and safe water source will unlock potential by returning time for our girls to study, for our mums to work and will also increase food production hence fighting other problems silently.

We work in Kakelo dudi in kasipul location south kabondo, Kotieno primary in Oyugis and Kanam village respectively in collaboration with Armstrong for girls and woman empowerment.
